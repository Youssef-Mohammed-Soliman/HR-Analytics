## 🔍 Project Overview

This project analyzes a company's HR dataset to uncover patterns in employee attrition, demographics, job roles, and salary distribution. The goal is to help HR teams make data-driven decisions to reduce turnover and improve workforce planning.

## 📌 Key Findings

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Total Employees | 1,480 |
| Attrition Rate | **16%** (238 employees) |
| Average Monthly Salary | **$6,505** |
| Total Monthly Payroll | **$9.63M** |
| Average Age | **37 years** |
| Largest Department | Research & Development (65%) |

### Top Insights
- 🔴 **Attrition** is highest among Sales Representatives and younger age groups (18–25)
- 👥 **Gender split**: 60% Male / 40% Female across all departments
- 💼 **R&D dominates** with 967 out of 1,480 employees
- ✈️ **70% of employees** do not travel for business
- 🎓 **Life Sciences & Medical** account for 73% of education backgrounds

## 📊 Dashboard Features

The Excel dashboard includes:
- **KPI Cards**: Total employees, payroll, avg salary, avg age, attrition rate
- **Filter Panel**: Slice by Gender, Department, and Age Group
- **Charts**: Attrition rate, department breakdown, travel status, gender split, job role distribution, education field, and age group distribution
